Why Choose Us

Biography/Mission

St David started in september 2015 with just three students, it has been a progressive journey since then. we have grown massively because of the quality of education we give to our students in both theory and practicals. With our best qualified and experienced staff, we have trained over 2000 students who are scattered all over the globe saving and impacting lives. St David has been a place of hope, where we bring out the best in our students, nuturing their dreams into reality. we can't say much but our products do speak for us. We have branches in Limbe, Buea, and Douala.

Vision

Becoming a world-class University to raise to tier industry experts for the emergence of global societies.

Core Values. These are the aspects that defines St David and it is reflected in our vision and belifts: Discipline Atmosphere, Research and Innovation, Positive Networking, World Class Teaching Quality, International Outlook, Awards and Distinction Focus.

Our Objectives. Develop and disseminate quality professional programs to raise professional experts who can engage into global competition, Building Professional skills in our students with the notion of not just to take over the job market but to be employers, Promotion of networking attitude, social cohesion, and discipline, Encourage creativity, professionalism, and technological exploits for national and international distinctions, Encourage research and e-learning to meet todays need.
